Reconnect - Contact Management App

Demo Video
In collaboration with Thiyaga B

Too many contacts, but not enough real connections? Over time, our contact lists get cluttered with old numbers, forgotten names, and people we once knew but never reached out to again. Manually cleaning it up? Too much effort.

That's why I built Reconnect—an interactive app that helps you organize, clean, and reconnect with meaningful contacts, all with a Tinder-style swipe UI.

Features

  • Swipe Right – Keep contacts you're interested in maintaining
  • Swipe Left – Remove contacts you no longer need
  • Swipe Up – Mark friends you want to catch up with soon
  • Bulk Organization – Group similar contacts and delete outdated ones in bulk
  • Memory Sharing – Makes reaching out after a long time feel more natural
  • Contact Integration – Seamlessly integrated with your local contacts list

Tech Stack

  • Kotlin
  • Android SDK
  • MVVM Architecture
  • Room Database
  • Contacts API
  • Material Design
  • Custom Animations
  • Coroutines

I wanted a simple way to track my goals, so I built a Goal Tracker App while learning more about Android development. Along the way, I got to explore:

Features

  • Firebase Authentication – For user sign-in
  • Firestore Database – To store and manage goals

Why This Project?

Sometimes, it's hard to stay consistent with goals. I thought having an app that tracks progress and shows streaks might help keep the motivation going.

If you're trying to build habits or stay on track with your goals, this app might help. The daily check-ins and calendar streaks make it easier to stay consistent and see progress over time.
